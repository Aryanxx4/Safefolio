Safefolio is India’s first one-stop platform for learning, practicing, and mastering trading and investing without the fear of losing real money.

Problem Statement
In India, trading and investing can seem intimidating to newcomers.
Most platforms focus on live trading,not learning leaving beginners vulnerable to losses before they understand core concepts like order types, risk management, or portfolio diversification.

Our Solution
Learn: Interactive modules teach trading, investing, and portfolio basics.
Practice: Use ₹10 lakh in virtual funds to trade real Indian stocks.
Simulate: Replay key market events like the 2008 crash or 2020 rally using historical market data.
Analyze: Review performance metrics such as P&L, Sharpe Ratio, and drawdown in a detailed analytics dashboard.

Safefolio bridges the gap between theory and experience all without the fear of losing real money.

Core Features
•⁠  ⁠Virtual Portfolio: Trade Indian stocks, crypto, and forex using demo funds to build confidence before entering real markets.
•⁠  ⁠Market Replay: Relive major events like the 2020 COVID rally or the 2008 financial crisis and test your strategies.
•⁠  ⁠Analytics Dashboard: Track P&L, win rate, Sharpe ratio, and other performance metrics.
•⁠  ⁠Watchlist: Keep an eye on your favorite stocks and get notified about key price movements or updates.

User Flow
1.⁠ ⁠Sign Up: Create an account and get ₹1,00,000 in virtual funds.
2.⁠ ⁠Learn: Go through interactive tutorials covering the basics of trading and investing.
3.⁠ ⁠Trade: Place buy/sell orders using simulated or real-time Indian market data.
4.⁠ ⁠Analyze: Review your portfolio performance with advanced analytics and insights.
5.⁠ ⁠Improve: Replay markets, test strategies, and refine your decision-making skills.

Challenges Faced

1.⁠ ⁠Integrating Real-Time Data APIs: Connecting Indian market APIs was challenging few provide real-time data affordably.
2.⁠ ⁠Connecting Backend with Database: Designing a robust backend architecture that securely interacts with the database was a key challenge.
3.⁠ ⁠Data Quality and Accuracy : Ensuring accurate, split-adjusted, and timely market data was crucial for maintaining realistic simulations and user trust.
